## What it is

The Homeland Security Exercise & Evaluation Program (HSEEP) is FEMA's
standardized methodology for designing, conducting, evaluating, and improving
exercises. HSEEP is the de facto standard for public-sector cybersecurity
tabletops.

## How Annual Tabletop maps to HSEEP

Every Annual Tabletop session follows the HSEEP cycle: design → conduct →
evaluate → improve. The AAR is HSEEP-conformant out of the box, with the
required sections (executive summary, exercise overview, analysis of
capabilities, conclusion, IPP) generated by Atlas based on session decisions.

## Capability targets

Scenarios are mapped to HSEEP capability targets where applicable, so your AAR
flows directly into your jurisdiction's Multi-Year Training and Exercise Plan
(MYTEP).
