## What it is

HIPAA Security Rule §164.308(a)(7)(ii)(D) requires covered entities and
business associates to implement procedures for periodic testing and revision
of contingency plans. Annual Tabletop's healthcare scenarios are designed to
generate the documented evidence OCR auditors expect.

## What the AAR captures

Each healthcare-segment AAR captures: contingency plan invoked, decisions
made, gaps identified, and the IPP for closure. The artifact is OCR-ready in
PDF and DOCX.
