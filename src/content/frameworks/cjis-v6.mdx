## What it is

CJIS Security Policy v6.0 governs the protection of criminal justice
information across federal, state, local, and tribal agencies. Section 5.3
covers Incident Response and the obligation to notify the CJIS Information
Security Officer.

## How Annual Tabletop maps to CJIS

Scenarios that exercise CJIS workflows include the v6.0 notification chain in
the inject sequence and document the chain-of-custody decisions in the AAR.
